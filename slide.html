Exploring Gender Inequality: A Decade of Change in Human Development

An Interactive Visualization of Gender Development and Human Development Indices (2012-2022)

Gender Development Index vs Human Development Index (2012-2022)

This interactive bubble chart visualizes countries' positions based on Gender Development Index (GDI) and Human Development Index (HDI). The size of each bubble represents the population of the country, and colors denote continents. You can explore annual changes from 2012 to 2022.

How to Use

Use the timeline to navigate through the years. Click on the 'Play' button to see how countries' positions evolve over time. The chart updates to show shifts in gender and human development, reflecting progress or setbacks.

Key Insights

Over the past decade, we can observe significant improvements in some regions, while others lag behind.

Certain countries have made remarkable strides in gender equality, closing gaps in human development indices.

Persistent gaps and disparities in GDI and HDI reveal areas where targeted interventions are needed.
